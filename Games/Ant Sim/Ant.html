I made the following changes to add a nest: 1. Added a `nest` variable to store the position of the nest. It's set to the center of the canvas in the `setup()` function. 2. The ants now start at the nest position in the `Ant` constructor. 3. When an ant is carrying food, it now moves directly towards the nest, instead of just moving in the opposite direction. This is done by calculating a vector from the ant's position to the nest, and setting the ant's velocity to this vector. 4. When an ant reaches the nest (i.e., gets within 10 pixels of it), it drops the food and a new food source is randomly placed on the canvas. 5. The nest is drawn as a brown circle in the `draw()` function. Now the ants will bring the food they find back to the central nest location. When they reach the nest, they'll drop the food and go out to search for more. The nest serves as a hub that the ants radiate out from and return to. Let me know if you have any other questions!
